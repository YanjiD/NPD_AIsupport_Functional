Core Product

Active Cabin Comfort Module (ACCM)

The ACCM integrates cabin thermal management, airflow distribution, noise and vibration suppression, and multisensory coordinated control to dynamically regulate comfort levels and experiential consistency within new energy vehicle cabins across driving conditions, environmental contexts, and usage scenarios.

Product Objective

Designed for Control and Functional Stability

Our ACCM is designed for cabin environment control and functional stability, with a focus on:

  • Control accuracy
  • System stability
  • Predictable performance

across operating conditions. The module aims to achieve a reliable balance among energy efficiency, while supporting robustness and consistency in mass production.

Design Decision Approach

Human Expertise, Augmented by AI

In our NPD process, AI is used as an advanced analytical tool to support engineering judgment. We apply AI to large-scale simulation and early risk identification, while experienced engineers remain directly responsible for evaluating trade-offs, finalizing parameters, and approving release decisions. We believe that expert review and clear documentation of design rationales are essential for effective collaboration.
